About My Custard Apple Farm

Custard apple is a poor man's apple but its taste is loved my many. This fruit season is from September to October in South India and October to December in North India.

MY FARM

I have a 3-acres of custard apple farm, about 150 Kms from Hyderabad, at Warangal. Have a variety of Balanagar as well as NMK1. We have around 900 plants with spacing of 16ft by 8 ft. The plantation was done on July 2016. Expecting fruits in 2018.

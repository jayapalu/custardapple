FAQ

1. How many acres of custard apples planted ?

Three acres

2. What is distance followed for the custard apple plantion ?

We followed 8 by 16 feet. The distance between each row is 16 feet and distance between the plants is 8 feet.

3. Variety of custard planted and when ?

Below custard apple variety planted and planted on end of July 2016

1. NMK-1

2. Balanagar

4. Where to buy the custard apple saplings ?

1. NMK-1 from Barshi, Maharastra

2. Balanagar from fruit research center, SangaReddy, Telangana

4. What is the cost per custard apple plant/saplings ?

1. NMK-1 Rs.70 each

2. Balanagar Rs.20 each, Telangana govt nursery

5. What care do I need after plantation ?

After plantation for the first two years canopy mangement care required to shape the plants.

Later on prunning of plants is needed

5. What is the schedule for custard apple plants management ?

Refer the Custard apple guide in resources section
